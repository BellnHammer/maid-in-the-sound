Our Story

Built by Builders.
Cleaned to a Higher Standard.

Maid in the Sound grew out of Bell & Hammer, a local contracting company we built right here in Kitsap County. That background shows up in everything we do. We know how a house comes together, which surfaces need careful handling after install, and what it actually takes to leave a space clean without leaving a mess behind. That kind of knowledge is not something you pick up on the job.

We are not a franchise. There is no corporate playbook, no regional manager somewhere else setting the bar. This is our community. Poulsbo, Silverdale, Bremerton, Port Orchard, Bainbridge Island. Every job has our name on it here, and we treat it that way.

Whether you need someone to keep your home running smoothly week to week or a thorough clean after a big project wraps up, you will work with real people who take pride in the work.
